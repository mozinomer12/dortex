About Us

QUALITY AND SERVICE - MADE IN GERMANY

We produce high-quality, personalized textile labels, leather labels, wooden labels, customized gift ribbon and freely designed decorative ribbons from a variety of materials. And have been doing so since 1986.

Our online shop, which is available in German, English, Dutch, Spanish, Italian, French, Russian and Polish, is testament to our customer-oriented production, user-friendly ordering process and open approach to communications.

What is important to us

Personal service and competent consultation

Our support team always has an open ear to the needs of our customers. Seven days a week, 16 hours a day. Our service hotline is free to use in Germany, Austria and Switzerland (Freecall 0800 0700 110, Mon.-Sun. 6.00 to 22.00).

High quality

All DORTEX products are made in German production facilities using high-quality materials. Our labels and satin ribbons are skin-friendly, colorfast and free of hazardous substances (Certification).

Great design freedom and simple ordering

Our customers are people with creative ideas and high demands. It is our task to record and implement these ideas in as detailed a way as possible. For this reason, we constantly work to improve loom controllers, ribbon printing machines and the functionality of our online shop. Our ordering wizard enables our customers to adapt fonts and motifs according to requirement. Own designs for woven labels or satin ribbons can be uploaded directly into the online shop and optimized in the preview.

Short delivery times

By optimizing all production and mailing procedures, we are able to keep delivery times as short as possible. To aid this, the basket in our online shop is linked directly to the production facilities. Loom and printing machine utilization are both optimized by the latest control software. All of our products are made in Germany. Here, you can take a look at the production of our woven cotton labels: to the video

Satisfied customers

We are happy to receive suggestions from our customers. For this reason, we place special emphasis on direct communication. Our company pages on social networks, participation in discussion forums on relevant topic-related portals and the independent evaluation tool from Trusted Shops help us to do this (to the evaluations).

Production in small quantities

Anyone who wishes to label their own crafting items or the clothing of their children will often not need more than 25 individual name tapes or woven labels. To create a very personal gift with a custom-designed gift ribbon, even 20cm may be more than sufficient. Not a problem for us!

German production

Production at a German site ensures both transparency and security. This also applies to the materials used but also to the production conditions. Thanks to short delivery routes, resources are used gently, delivery times are reduced and mailing costs minimized.
